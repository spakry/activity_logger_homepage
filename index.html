At the end of the day, have you ever forgotten what you did, or find it takes a lot of time to remember? Wished you had an automatic log like a server to analyze your workflows? This smart daemon silently capturing your high‑level actions in the background. Every meaningful task gets logged automatically, creating a complete record of your day that you can summarize, review, and use to optimize your workflow patterns.

What you get

  • Never forget your accomplishments – automatically logs every meaningful action so you always know what you've done. No more "what did I do today?" moments! 🎯
  • Daily summaries & insights – get instant summaries of your work patterns and productivity trends from your logged actions. It's like having a productivity coach! 📊
  • Workflow optimization – analyze your logged activities to identify inefficiencies and optimize how you work. Work smarter, not harder! 💪
  • Complete memory backup – search your personal activity timeline to find exactly what you worked on and when. Your personal Google for your work! 🔍
  • Privacy first – your logged data stays local and only you control what gets shared. Your data, your rules! 🔒
